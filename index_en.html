KryptoStone

KryptoStone aims to become the first real estate crypto-asset in France and around the world. Whether you are scholars or novices in the web 3.0 universe, KryptoStone offers you the opportunity to diversify your investments, by offering a tangible solution combining real estate, crypto-assets and NFT. Thanks to Blockchain technology, it is now possible to invest in real estate. And this, regardless of the size of your wallet.

our services

Smart contracts, smart buildings, smarter cities

NFTs

Through these new technologies, Krypto Stone offers the greatest number of people to become owners of real estate through the acquisition of NFT (Non Fungible Token). A non-fungible token (NFT) is by definition unique and benefits its holder

Digital assets

Buildings identified and validated by a recognized family property company which are tokenized: the value of the properties, their quality, their profitability and their interests in relation to the market are guaranteed by the expertise of a family property company which has proven itself.

Sustainable approach

Whatever the property purchased, if work has to be carried out, it is always with a concern for sustainable and societal development. Our team of sustainable development experts strives to make each renovation neutral and virtuous.

Web3 training

Because the development of the web3 sector in France can only be done with competent and well-trained actors, Kryptostone is banking on the talents of tomorrow by opening one of the first national web3 and blockchain training centers.

Why Metz

With KRYPTO STONE and the other locally established blockchain companies, there is no doubt that the Eurometropolis of Metz will become the European capital of blockchain and once again become a central place for new technologies!

Why Elrond

Elrond, the first carbon-negative blockchain in the world, is a complete, innovative, secure, accessible and virtuous European ecosystem. It reflects our values so much that we couldn't build in a better environment.
